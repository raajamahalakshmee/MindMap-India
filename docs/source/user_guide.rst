Overview
--------

Mindmap India is an AI-powered career exploration tool that helps users discover career paths based on their interests, skills, and preferences. The application uses machine learning to analyze career data and provide personalized recommendations.

Key Features
-----------

- **Career Exploration**: Browse and search through a comprehensive database of careers
- **Personalized Recommendations**: Get AI-powered career suggestions based on your interests
- **Detailed Career Information**: View in-depth information about each career, including required skills and education
- **Comparison Tool**: Compare different careers side by side
- **Skill Gap Analysis**: Identify skills you need to develop for your target career

Using the Application
---------------------

### Home Page

The home page provides an overview of the application and quick access to key features:

1. **Search Bar**: Search for specific careers or skills
2. **Featured Careers**: Browse popular or trending careers
3. **Quick Start Guide**: Get started with a guided tour of the application

### Exploring Careers

1. **Browse Careers**
   - Click on the "Browse Careers" button to see all available careers
   - Use filters to narrow down by industry, salary range, education level, etc.
   - Sort careers by popularity, salary, or growth potential

2. **View Career Details**
   - Click on any career to see detailed information
   - View required skills, education, salary information, and job outlook
   - See related careers and career paths

3. **Get Recommendations**
   - Take the career assessment quiz to get personalized recommendations
   - Or, select careers you're interested in to find similar options
   - Save careers to your profile for future reference

### Career Comparison

1. Select up to 3 careers to compare
2. View side-by-side comparisons of key metrics
3. Save or export comparison results

### User Profile

Create an account to:
- Save favorite careers
- Track your career exploration progress
- Receive personalized recommendations
- Set career goals and milestones

### Advanced Features

- **Skill Gap Analysis**: Upload your resume or list your skills to see how they match with different careers
- **Education Pathways**: Explore educational requirements and recommended courses
- **Job Market Insights**: View employment trends and growth projections

Tips for Effective Career Exploration
------------------------------------

1. **Be Open-Minded**: Explore a variety of careers, even those you may not have considered before
2. **Research Thoroughly**: Look beyond job titles to understand day-to-day responsibilities
3. **Consider Your Values**: Think about what's important to you in a career (salary, work-life balance, job security, etc.)
4. **Talk to Professionals**: Reach out to people working in fields that interest you
5. **Gain Experience**: Look for internships, volunteer opportunities, or job shadowing experiences

Troubleshooting
---------------

### Common Issues

- **Page not loading**: Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache
- **Recommendations not relevant**: Make sure to provide detailed information in your profile and assessment
- **Missing careers**: Our database is regularly updated, but if you notice any missing careers, please let us know
